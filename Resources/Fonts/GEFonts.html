Fonts of Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge

This collection of fan-made fonts is designed to help you create new and interesting objects, props, and art based in the world created by Walt Disney Imagineering and Lucas Films in Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge in Disneyland Park and Disney's Hollywood Studios at Walt Disney World.

The Typefaces

  • BatuuBesh

    BatuuBesh is a typeface based on the handwritten Aurebesh that guests find scattered around the Black Spire Outpost. The glyphs are based on a handwritten Aurebesh that appeared in Star Wars: the Visual Encyclopedia (2017).

    Jar Jar Binks quietly removed his X Wing fez cap.


  • Batuu Chalkboard

    This typeface is based on the chalkboard-style fonts used for the menu boards in Docking Bay 7 Food and Cargo. The glyphs have a distinct hand-drawn feel, reflecting Strono "Cookie" Tuggs' one-week-a-month itinerant tenancy of in Black Spire Outpost.

    The Emperor cackled whilst bequeathing Vader a jazzy fox.
